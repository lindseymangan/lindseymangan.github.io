Media Production

The Art of Dance

×

The photo series assignment was a final project for my Photojournalism class. We were given the task to select a topic of our choosing based on a passion, hobby, or event. As a previous dancer as one point, I chose to photograph the University of St. Thomas Dance Club as they prepared for their Spring Showcase.

×

My intention behind this photo series was to capture the art, athleticism, and time that goes into dance. Especially since dance as a whole is very commonly not associated as a sport.

×

This was an individual project that I was in charge of all the roles. This included planning, photographing, developing, editing, and presenting the entirety of the series.

×

I used a Canon camera, shot in manual mode, RAW format, and developed/edited in Adobe Lightroom.

Vintage Clothing Market

×

This series of photos was also for my Photojournalism class. The task was to go somewhere interesting and find interesting people and things to photograph. The title of the assigment was "Content is King".

×

I decided to complete this project through photographs from the Minneapolis Vintage Market at Quincy Hall in North East Minneapolis.

×

I went around for hours capturing different vendor stands and their products. Items such as clothing, purses, belts, shoes, jewelry, and more were in every corner!

×

This was an individual project. I learned a lot from this experience, met some wonderful people, and got to look through an array of vintage clothing and other items.

Wedding Ceremony & Reception

×

These set of photos were taken at a wedding ceremony and reception for a family friend. This was towards the beginning of my photography practices but she wanted as many pictures of their day as possible and I got to help out.

×

The photos I have included for my portfolio are my favorites of the album that I captured on their wedding day. Small moments, big moments, and all of the in between.

×

Weddings are very important to all those involved and this opportunity was such a crucial learning point in my media production career.
