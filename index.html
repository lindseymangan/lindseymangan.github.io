Who am I?

I'm Lindsey Mangan, an up-and-coming Media Designer with strengths in areas of graphic design, branding, photography, and social media. I feel magnetized towards projects involving music, fashion, architecture, paintings, and so much more. You will find an array of color incorporated throughout my portfolio pieces, as nothing tells stories and emotion like color. I dabble with a varying balance of animations/graphics and real-life content, because who wants everything to be the same all the time! I am looking forward to pursuing a variety of opportunities in whatever creative direction my next project leads me to.
