Drowning in Danger - Virtual Art Gallery

This project was completed for an Art History class, Contemporary Art: Visual Representation of the Mediterranean. The course was completed while abroad in Aix en Provence, France. For this gallery, we got to choose a topic of our choice, but it had to have relation to the Mediterranean Sea. As we had learned about migration history from all over the Sea throughout the class, it was a topic that stood out to me most, especially when incorporating the elements of physical art as well. There were many working parts that went into this project, including researching to find relative art pieces to the theme and ones that were considered contemporary. Another working part that went into it, was the curation process of the space. We got to pick out the general space design but then make it our own with each art piece and its placement. It was required to include a full description of only two of the pieces, rather than all of them so I chose two that I really enjoyed learning about. Please pause as needed throughout the video to take a longer look as the pieces.
